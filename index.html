Playing it: every pill is a live string — tap to pluck, sweep down the strings to strum, drag along a string to slide (release lands on the nearest fret). In Bar mode, drag the steel and the whole ringing chord glides with it. Sharps: shown with ♯ (e.g. F♯). Each sharp is also the flat of the next note (F♯ = G♭) — same fret, two names.
